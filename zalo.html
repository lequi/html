Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ZaloShop Builder

Do I need coding skills to use ZaloShop Builder?

No coding skills are required! Our drag-and-drop builder makes it easy for anyone to create a professional Zalo mini app store. If you can use basic computer programs, you can build your store with our platform.

How is this different from a regular Zalo OA store?

Zalo mini apps provide a much richer, more interactive shopping experience than basic OA store features. Your store will look and function like a native mobile app with better performance, more design flexibility, and advanced ecommerce features.

What payment methods can I accept?

We support all major payment methods in Vietnam including ZaloPay, credit/debit cards, bank transfer, and cash on delivery (COD). You can enable/disable any payment method in your store settings.

Can I migrate from another platform?

Yes! We provide tools to import your product catalog from Shopify, WooCommerce, Haravan, and other platforms. Our support team can also assist with migration if needed.

What happens if I need help?

We offer 24/7 email support for all plans, with live chat and phone support available on higher tiers. Our Vietnamese-speaking support team is knowledgeable about both our platform and Zalo's ecosystem.
