Step 1: Create TikTok Shop Account

To start dropshipping on TikTok Shop, you first need to create a seller account. Visit the TikTok Shop website or download the TikTok Seller app from your app store. Follow the registration process, providing all required business information and documents.

Step 2: Open TTS Dropship App

Download and install the TTS Dropship app from your app store. Once installed, open the app and navigate to the "Account" section. This is your control center for managing your dropshipping business.

Step 3: Link TikTok Shop Store

In the TTS Dropship app, go to "Sell on TikTok Shop" and select "Add TikTok Shop Store". You'll need to authorize the connection between your TikTok Shop account and the dropshipping app. This allows you to manage products and orders seamlessly.

Step 4: Select Supplier

Choose a reliable supplier to link with your store. The TTS Dropship app provides a list of verified suppliers. Review their product offerings, shipping times, and ratings before making your selection. You can link with multiple suppliers to diversify your product catalog.

Check ratings and reviews

Step 5: Product Listing

Navigate to the product list from your supplier. Select products you want to sell and click "List Product". Customize the product title, description, and price for your store. The app will automatically sync inventory and handle order processing.

Set your profit margin here

Step 6: Order & Payment Process

When a customer places an order on your TikTok Shop, you'll receive notification. The supplier will ship directly to your customer. You only need to pay the supplier after receiving payment from TikTok Shop (typically after order fulfillment).

Step 7: Receive Profit

Your profit is the difference between the price you set on TikTok Shop and the supplier's price. After deducting any platform fees, your earnings will be deposited to your account. Monitor your sales analytics in the app to optimize your product selection and pricing strategy.
